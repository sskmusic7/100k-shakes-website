## 1. Key Insights from the 100K Shakes Brand Deck

### Vision and Mission
100K Shakes positions itself as a fusion of indulgent milkshakes and premium alcohol, aiming to "redefine indulgence" through rich flavours, luxurious ingredients and innovative twists. The mission: elevate the classic milkshake with premium ingredients, create memorable Instagram-worthy experiences and cater to diverse tastes (non-alcoholic and alcohol-infused).

### Product Range
- **StraightShakes**: Signature flavours like Oreo, Milo and Strawberry
- **ShotShakes**: Luxury alcohol-infused shakes with spirits (Jägermeister, Amarula)
- **Ice Cream Cones**: Classic indulgent treats

### Brand Identity
- **Tone**: Friendly, adventurous and knowledgeable
- **Logo**: Playful, stylised milkshake cup
- **Colours**: Deep chocolate brown, strawberry red, milky off-white
- **Typography**: Modern fonts blended with handwritten-style script
- **Imagery**: High-quality, mouth-watering milkshake photos

### Target Audience
Millennials, Gen Z, foodies and adults seeking premium desserts and alcohol-infused indulgences

### Competitive Edge
Alcohol-infused milkshakes differentiate the brand. Marketing emphasizes vibrant online presence, influencer collaborations, events, loyalty rewards and seasonal promotions.

### Growth Strategy
- Mall kiosks
- Franchising opportunities
- International expansion
- Eco-friendly practices
- Vegan/dairy-free options

### Common Design Patterns

#### Hero Imagery & Taglines
Every successful site leads with high-impact visuals (photos/videos) and catchy taglines that encapsulate the brand promise.

#### Clear Navigation
Simple top menus organize key sections:
- Menu/Products
- Locations
- Franchise Information
- Brand Story
- Contact

#### Product Showcases
Sites highlight product categories with:
- Enticing photos
- Brief descriptions
- Filters or quick-view cards
- Category organization

#### Storytelling & Values
Brands explain their heritage, ingredient quality and preparation methods to build trust and connection.

#### Strong Calls-to-Action
Prominent buttons encourage visitors to:
- Order online
- View full menus
- Subscribe to newsletters
- Join rewards programmes
- Explore franchising

#### Extra Offerings
- Loyalty schemes
- Gift cards
- Merchandise
- Signature challenges (e.g., "Gallon Challenge")
- Event bookings

## 5. Image Placeholder Instructions

### Directory Structure
Create an `images` folder with these placeholders:

```
/images/
  ├── hero-placeholder.jpg (1920x1080px minimum)
  ├── menu-item-placeholder.jpg (800x800px, repeated per product)
  ├── story-placeholder.jpg (1200x800px)
  ├── shotshake-placeholder.jpg (1000x1000px)
  └── sustainability-placeholder.jpg (1200x800px)
```

### Replacement Guidelines
When adding real images:
1. Maintain aspect ratios shown above
2. Optimize for web (compress without losing quality)
3. Use descriptive alt-text for accessibility
4. Consider WebP format for better performance
5. Include @2x versions for retina displays
