## General Image Generation Tips

### Consistency Elements (Use Template)
- Clear/transparent cup showing shake contents
- Branded straw (colors vary per item)
- Professional studio lighting
- Clean white or neutral background
- Appetizing, mouth-watering presentation
- Condensation droplets on cup exterior

### Quality Settings
- High resolution (minimum 1200x1200px)
- Sharp focus on toppings and texture
- Bright, vibrant colors
- Professional food photography style
- Slight overhead angle (45 degrees)

### Branding Elements
- 100K Shakes logo placement (optional)
- "21+" badge for ShotShakes
- "V" or leaf symbol for vegan items
- Consistent cup and straw branding
