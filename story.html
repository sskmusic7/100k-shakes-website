Our Story

From a Bramley gazebo to redefining indulgence

2020

The Beginning

100K Shakes started as a dream in a Bramley gazebo, where we first experimented with combining premium milkshakes and innovative flavours. Our founders believed that indulgence could be elevated beyond the ordinary.

2021

First Pop-up Success

Our first pop-up events introduced the concept of alcohol-infused milkshakes to the market. The response was overwhelming, and we knew we were onto something special.

2022

Expanding Horizons

We opened our first permanent locations and introduced our signature StraightShakes line. The brand began to gain recognition for its premium ingredients and Instagram-worthy presentations.

2023

Innovation & Growth

ShotShakes became our signature differentiator. We expanded to mall kiosks, launched our loyalty programme, and introduced vegan options to make indulgence accessible to everyone.

2024

Redefining Indulgence

Today, 100K Shakes continues to push boundaries with new flavours, sustainable practices, and a commitment to creating memorable experiences. We're not just serving milkshakes‚Äîwe're elevating indulgence.
